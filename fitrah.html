Deen al-Fitrah: The Natural Law School of Jurisprudence

In this masterwork, we journey through a profound discourse of spiritual understanding, delving into the depths of the Deen al-Fitrah, the natural religion, as elucidated in the al-Quran. We cast a critical gaze upon the Deen al-Islam, finding it to be but a shadow of the true spiritual essence enshrined in the original messages of the Quran. We illuminate the way this elite-guided interpretation has eclipsed the Deen al-Fitrah and stifled the practice of Natural Law.

Over thirteen insightful sections, we explore the sacred corridors of divine understanding, unearthing the wisdom of the 99 divine names of Allah and establishing the foundations of Validity, Truth, and Soundness. We delve into the symbology of the classical elements - Fire, Air, Earth, and Water - revealing their roles as keys to understanding these divine attributes.

As we traverse the intricate tapestry of existence, we recognize the harmonious symphony of contracts between energies, offering an enlightening perspective on the universal laws of energy. We present the Deen al-Fitrah not merely as a set of laws or principles, but as a holistic way of life that integrates spiritual understanding, intellectual balance, and ethical action.

In the heart of this work, we shine a light on the Global Charter of Ecospirituality, a beacon calling humanity to unite under the Green State and establish a system based on the Deen al-Fitrah. We explore the principles of the gift economy, contrasting this natural law-based economic system with the current monetary system of enslavement.

Our journey culminates in an impassioned call to reestablish the natural order, to awaken the Deen al-Fitrah, and to return to a system of Common Law Jurisprudence. This discourse serves as an anthem for the resurgence of the Natural Law, a clarion call to those yearning for a world guided by the principles of truth, justice, and spiritual enlightenment.
