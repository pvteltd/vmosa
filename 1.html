Naturalist Constitution
By Ecospiritualist

Introduction

In this illuminating exploration of humanity's ongoing struggle against Lucifer's deceptions, we will delve into three crucial aspects that hold the key to our spiritual salvation. The first section will focus on the cosmic battle between Lucifer and the Children of Adam, shedding light on the origins of the conflict and the stakes involved. The second section will discuss the essential elements of the divine covenant, emphasizing the importance of ecospirituality, religious naturalism, and spiritual naturalism in preserving the Earth and upholding our sacred responsibilities. Finally, in the third section, we will examine the deceptive tactics employed by Lucifer to manipulate and control humanity, revealing the crucial need for awareness and steadfastness in the face of these challenges.

The Luminous Journey Within:
Unveiling the Essence of Our True Nature

In the tapestry of existence, the Divine has woven a profound mystery that connects us all. The nature of this connection is illuminated by the wisdom of the Quran, as it guides us through the cosmic dance of light and darkness. In this article, we explore the interplay between humanity, the Divine, and the enigmatic figure of Lucifer, as we seek to unravel the inner journey that defines our existence.

At the heart of this mystery lies the essence of God, the Eternal Energy that sustains all life and permeates the entire universe. As the Quran beautifully describes in verse 24:35, God is the Light that illuminates our path and dispels the darkness of ignorance. This divine energy radiates within each of us, as we are both light upon light - positive and negative, forming a perfect balance in our quest for spiritual harmony.

However, the life of this world can often seem like a mere illusion (verse 57:20), clouding our perception and distancing us from our true nature. It is only through a journey within ourselves (verse 53:1-15) that we can transcend this illusory veil and uncover the eternal connection we share with the Divine.

In the celestial realm, the figure of Lucifer emerges as the leader of angels (verse 2:30). Created from fire or light (verses 18:50 and 15:27), Lucifer's radiance is unparalleled, yet pride leads him to forget that he, too, is light upon light, a manifestation of the Divine. This tragic forgetfulness sets the stage for a cosmic conflict, as God creates mankind from the earth (verse 15:28) with the intention of making them the Kings on Earth (verses 2:30 and 15:29-30).

All angels, including Lucifer, are commanded to prostrate before mankind in recognition of their inherent nobility (verse 15:30). However, Lucifer refuses (verses 15:31-33), arrogantly asserting his superiority and challenging God's decision to elevate humanity (verses 2:30 and 2:34). Consequently, Lucifer becomes the enemy of mankind and the Children of Adam.

Fueled by his pride and resentment, Lucifer vows to mislead humanity, seeking to prove that God's decision to make mankind the Kings on Earth was flawed. In this struggle between light and darkness, the stage is set for a profound spiritual journey - one that transcends the material world and delves into the depths of our souls.

As we embark on this luminous journey within, let us remember that our true nature is a reflection of the Divine Light. By embracing our innate connection to God, we can rise above the illusion of this world and reclaim our rightful place as the Kings on Earth. In doing so, we can overcome the challenges posed by Lucifer and fulfill our divine purpose, shining as beacons of light in the vast expanse of the cosmos.

Embracing the Covenant of Ecospirituality:
A Journey Towards Harmony with Nature and the Divine

As we continue our exploration of the interconnectedness between humanity, the Divine, and our environment, the story of a sacred covenant unfolds. This covenant, offered by God to the heavens, the earth, and the mountains, represents an immense responsibility and trust (verses 33:72-73). Although these mighty forces of nature refused to bear this covenant out of fear of failing, mankind, in their audacity and courage, voluntarily accepted the divine trusteeship (verses 33:72-73).

By accepting this covenant, humanity was granted the power to be Kings on Earth, with the gift of free will and freedom (verse 2:30). The essence of this covenant lies in upholding the Sunnatullah or Natural Law and rejecting ideologies born from humanism and relativism. It is a reminder that we cannot walk with God while holding hands with Satan at the same time (verse 7:172).

The covenant also emphasizes the importance of embracing ecospirituality as the only true religion, transcending distinctions between naturalism, ecospiritualism, religious naturalism, and spiritual naturalism. This unifying worldview calls upon humanity to protect and preserve natural rights, as well as safeguarding the Earth for our future generations. As the adage goes, "We do not inherit the Earth from our ancestors; we borrow it from our children."

Within the sacred verses of the Quran (7:172 and 3:64), this covenant serves as the one and only constitution for humanity, outlining the guidelines for preserving a naturalistic way of life. The covenant can be divided into three essential sections, as described in verses 60:8-9.

The first part of the covenant calls for the defense of unalienable rights (verse 60:9), emphasizing the intrinsic value and dignity of every living being. The second part highlights the importance of protecting our land and homeland, recognizing the significance of nurturing a healthy relationship with our environment. Finally, the third part of the covenant urges us to refrain from engaging in political jurisprudence and supporting rule by law, as these human-made systems can often lead to the subversion of natural rights and ecological balance.

In conclusion, the sacred covenant offered to mankind serves as a powerful reminder of our responsibility to uphold the principles of ecospirituality and our interconnectedness with the Divine and the natural world. By embracing this covenant and striving to live in harmony with the Sunnatullah or Natural Law, we can create a world where unalienable rights are protected, the Earth is cherished, and the luminous essence of our true nature shines brightly in the cosmic dance of existence.

The Battle for Humanity's Soul:
Embracing Ecospirituality in the Face of Lucifer's Deceptions

As the ancient battle between Lucifer and the Children of Adam continues to unfold, it is essential to recognize the tactics employed by this cunning adversary. Lucifer, having vowed war against Adam and his progeny (verses 15:36-43), has skillfully utilized humanism as a means to lead mankind astray (verse 7:20).

Lucifer's first weapon is the power of hierarchy, symbolized metaphorically as a star (verse 6:76). This power represents the Law and Economic School of Jurisprudence, which often places the interests of the elite above the needs of the majority, sowing discord and perpetuating inequality.

The second weapon in Lucifer's arsenal is the power of schism, symbolized by the moon (verse 6:77). This power corresponds to the Historical/Sociological School of Jurisprudence, which fosters division by emphasizing the differences among various cultures, religions, and social groups.

The third and final weapon wielded by Lucifer is the power of materialism and consumerism, symbolized by the sun (verse 6:78). This power represents the Analytical Jurisprudence, which often reduces the value of life to mere material possessions, fostering greed and avarice.

Through these deceptive life values, Lucifer succeeded in leading Adam and Eve astray (verses 7:10-25). Consequently, humanity was sent to live in this simulation world, having failed to preserve the Earth and uphold the sacred covenant.

To reclaim our birthright and restore balance to the world, mankind must embrace the tenets of ecospirituality as outlined in the divine covenant. This path requires us to preserve the Earth according to the covenant's principles. Those who succeed in this endeavor will be granted entry to Heaven, while those who fail to uphold these sacred responsibilities will face the consequences of their actions in Hell.

In conclusion, humanity finds itself at a crucial crossroads. We can continue to succumb to the deceptions of Lucifer, perpetuating a world of hierarchy, division, and materialism, or we can choose to embrace the divine covenant of ecospirituality. By doing so, we can reclaim our true purpose as the Children of Adam and protectors of the Earth, ushering in an era of harmony, justice, and communion with the Divine.
